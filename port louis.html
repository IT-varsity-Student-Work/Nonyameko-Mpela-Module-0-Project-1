Port Louis(Mauritius)

Port Louis the capital of Mauritius, is one of the most popular destinations to visit in the country.It is a melting pot of glitz and glamour harmonising beautifully with traditions and customs from its 300 years old past.Port Louis is fascinating in terms of its mix in culture and traditions.Port Louis harbour, the port of the capital is the unique commerical harbour of Mauritius, it is well protected and sheltered from the south east trade winds by the mountain ranges, the port is a deep harbour.Inside the capital you will be overwhelmed by the intense activity that prevails across the boulevards and along the streets,you will come across alot of motorcycles, cars, and buses.Port Louis is the largest city of the island with approximately 200 000 inhabitants.Port Louis is the capital and also the economic centre point of our paradise island mauritius.Port louis was founded about 1736 by the french as a calling place for ships rounding the cape of good hope(south africa)on the passage between asia and europe.The city is the central collecting and clearing point for all imports and exports from mauritius and its dependecies and is connected by road to the rest of the island.The city is dominated by an old fortress the citadel(1838) built on a hill almost in the centre.
