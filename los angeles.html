Los Angeles

The city of Los Angeles holds many distinctions.Los Angeles is the entertainment capital of the world, a cultural mecca boasting more than 100 museums, many of them world class and a paradise of idyllic weather. Los Angeles is a place to be.Los Angeles is the largest city in California, with an estimated population of nearly four million people.It is the countries second most populous city(after new york city) and the third most populous city in north america(after mexico city).Los Angeles is know for its mediterranean climate, ethnic diversity,hollywood entertainment industry and its sprawling metropolis.Visit Los Angeles and you will surely fell inlove withit.The city sprawls across a broad coastal plain situated between mountains and the pacific ocean, the much longer los angeles country, which encompasses the city, contains some 900 other incorporated cities, uncluding beverly hills, pasadena and long beach. Los angeles the heart of southern california, became a world-class city very recently.At the start of the 20th century it was considered merely a large village. Los angeles is a place of extraordinary ethnic and racial diversity, owing largely to immigration and like other world cities, its reflects a growing gap between rich and poor.Los angeles has endured the barbs of many detractors.
